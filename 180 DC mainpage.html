WHO WE ARE

We are the VIT Chennai branch of 180 Degrees Consulting, the world's largest student-based consulting organization that provides socially conscious organizations with high- quality, affordable consulting services.

WHAT WE DO

We leverage the skills and expertise of the best students in India to problem solve and consult for organizations that create social impact. With interdisciplinary teams and an unwavering focus on social impact and innovation, we empower clients to create lasting change across the world.
